F.A.Q

Questions & answers

All account has a 60-minutes ($10) free trial.

How can I create a voice AI agent for my company?

Creating a voice AI agent with Retell is easy! Start by creating an account on the Retell dashboard. Each account comes with 60 minutes of free usage. Once logged in, you can build a voice agent and use our custom tools to integrate it with your company’s data seamlessly.

What is the pricing for Retell AI?

Retell AI offers a pay-as-you-go model starting at $0.07 per minute, depending on the selected voice and LLM configurations. We also offer an Enterprise Plan with reduced rates, custom concurrent call options, and premium support.

Can I connect the agent to my existing phone number?

Yes, you can! Retell supports integration with any telephony system through SIP trunking, making it easy to connect your existing phone number.

What resources does my company need to use Retell AI?

You’ll need one engineer to integrate Retell AI with your systems and create a voice AI agent. The initial setup typically takes anywhere from a few hours to a few days. Once the agent is set up, ongoing maintenance can be handled by a product manager or similar team member.
